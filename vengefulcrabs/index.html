The Vengeful Crabs

A first-person PvPvE experience where you and your fellow crewmates plunder the seas for treasure and fight other crews on the salty sea.

A first-person PvPvE experience with a heavy focus on PvP content. It's a punishing arena deathmatch where you and 1-2 teammates fight with other teams over the maps bounties.

A social deduction game where 2 of your 8 person crew are traitors. You will either work with your team to navigate the icy passage or work with your fellow thrall to sabotage the mission.

A PvE Co-op adventurer where you and your 3 teammates plunge into the depths of a cave system in pursuit of rare minerals. You'll have to work together to defeat the hordes of local insects who do NOT appreciate your intrusion!

A PvE Co-op side-scrolling adventure where you and your crew try to survive the depths of Europa in your submarine in this lovecraftian horror.
